Bowen University

The university was founded on 17 july 2001by the NBC in the old 1,300 acre campus of the Baptist College.

History

The Founder of Bowen University is late Rev. Thomas Jefferson Bowen. The idea of establishing a Baptist University in Nigeria was first conceived in 1938. This was borne out of the Baptist commitment to assist the people develop their potentials to the fullest. The bold step towards actualising this vision was not taken until 1956.
